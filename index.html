Barfi


Ingredients

  • Milk or Khoya (Mawa)
  • Ghee
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Prepare the base: Heat ghee in a pan, add khoya (or milk solids), and cook until it softens and starts to melt.
  2. Add sugar and flavor: Mix in sugar and stir continuously until it dissolves and the mixture thickens. Add any flavorings like cardamom or nuts if desired.
  3. Set and cool: Pour the mixture into a greased tray, spread evenly, and let it cool and set. Once firm, cut into pieces and serve.

Gulab Jamun


Ingredients

  • Khoya (Mawa) - the base ingredient for soft, rich texture
  • Maida (All-purpose flour) - used for binding
  • Sugar - to make the sweet sugar syrup (chashni)

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, maida, and a pinch of baking soda. Knead gently into a soft dough and shape into small smooth balls.
  2. Heat oil or ghee on low flame and deep-fry the balls until golden brown, stirring gently to cook evenly.
  3. Prepare warm sugar syrup with water, sugar, and cardamom. Soak the fried jamuns in the syrup for at least 1-2 hours.

Rasmalai


Ingredients

  • Paneer (Chenna or fresh cottage cheese)
  • Milk (full cream, boiled and reduced)
  • Sugar


Recipe

  1. Prepare soft paneer balls and cook them in warm sugar syrup until they expand.
  2. Boil milk with sugar and cardamom, stirring often, until it thickens into a creamy rabri.
  3. Soak the cooked paneer balls in the rabri for a few hours, then chill and serve.
